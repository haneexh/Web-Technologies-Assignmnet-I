Vardhaman College of Engineering (VCE), established in 1999,
                is promoted by the Vardhaman Educational Society,
                a not-for-profit organization committed to advancing quality education.
                The Society was founded with a vision to establish and manage institutions
                that impart education across diverse disciplines, including engineering,
                technology, sciences, management, and vocational training, while fostering
                creativity, innovation, and entrepreneurial skills among students.
                The Society operates on a non-profit and non-commercial basis, focusing on
                societal development and knowledge dissemination.Under the aegis of the
                Vardhaman Educational Society, VCE has emerged as a leading institution
                known for academic excellence, research contributions, and holistic student
                development. The institution continues to uphold the core objectives of the
                Society by integrating quality education, innovation, and social responsibility,
                thereby contributing significantly to the advancement of technical education
                and societal progress.
